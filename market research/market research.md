## Competitor Profiles

### RecipeTinEats (recipetineats.com)
- **Focus:** Fast-prep, big-flavor home cooking across global cuisines
- **Platform:** WordPress with WP Recipe Maker plugin
- **Monetization:** AdThrive (Raptive) ads, email-gated recipe printing, cookbook sales, Chicory ingredient shopping
- **Audience Scale:** Large — high comment counts (100–600+ per recipe)

### The Woks of Life (thewoksoflife.com)
- **Focus:** Chinese home cooking and restaurant-style recipes from a family of four cooks
- **Platform:** WordPress with WP Recipe Maker, wpDiscuz comments
- **Monetization:** AdThrive ads, cookbook sales, Mailchimp newsletter, brand partnerships
- **Audience Scale:** 1,600+ recipes, 150,000+ comments

### Food52 (food52.com)
- **Focus:** Editorial food content + curated e-commerce (kitchen, home, food products)
- **Platform:** Custom-built with integrated shop
- **Monetization:** E-commerce (2,000+ products), editorial sponsorships, newsletter
- **Audience Scale:** 13M monthly users, 4M+ Instagram followers

## Strategic Recommendations

### High Priority (Should Implement Next)
1. **Newsletter/email signup** — Every competitor has this. Critical for building a return audience. Consider offering a free guide (e.g., "10 Anti-Inflammatory Recipes" PDF) as a lead magnet.
2. **Nutrition facts** — Users actively search for this. Enables Google recipe rich snippets with nutrition data. Requires DB schema changes + admin form updates.

### Medium Priority
3. **Recipe video embeds** — High engagement format. Could start with embedding YouTube videos in recipes where relevant.
4. **Author bio on recipes** — Builds trust and personal connection, especially important for a site about traditional wisdom.
5. **Trust/social proof section** — Homepage stats showing recipe count, comment count, tradition systems covered.

### Low Priority (Future Consideration)
6. **Cookbook/e-book** — Natural extension once content library is large enough.
7. **Community submissions** — User-contributed recipes reviewed by editorial team.
8. **Ingredient shopping links** — Monetization through affiliate links for specialty ingredients.
9. **E-commerce** — Curated traditional cooking tools, specialty ingredients, or tea/spice collections.

### Leverage Your Differentiators
- **"Food is Medicine" positioning** is unique in the market — no competitor occupies this niche
- **Traditional medicine integration** (TCM, Ayurveda) creates content depth competitors can't easily replicate
- **Intelligent unit conversion** is technically superior to any competitor's approach
- **Medicinal tag filtering** is a feature users can't find anywhere else — promote it prominently
- **User taste profiles** enable future personalization (recommended recipes, customized collections) that competitors lack the data for
